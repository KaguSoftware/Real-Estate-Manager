in `promo/PromoVideo.tsx`.

you there's a better way than to a hard sell. Let the pauses breathe; the
anvils land in the gaps.

## Full read (for a voice actor)

> Scattered leads. Lost contracts. Paperwork piling up.
>
> There's a better way.
>
> Every property, in one place. Every lead, captured the moment it lands.
> Contracts, done in a click.
>
> Kagu Real Estate dot com. Real estate, simplified.

**Runtime:** ~22s of speech in a 30s slot — the gaps are deliberate.

## Recording notes

- Quiet room, phone 15–20cm from mouth, off-axis to avoid plosives.
- One take per section is fine; leave 2s of silence at the head.
- Deliver as `promo/audio/vo.mp3` (or .wav / .m4a) — Remotion accepts any of them.
- No need to match the timings exactly. Record it naturally; the composition
  can be nudged to the read rather than the other way round.
